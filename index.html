About Rand Choice

Rand Choice is a simple app crafted to assist you in making prompt decisions. Input your dilemma, add or import all available options, and utilize the spinner, slot machine, or treasure chest to swiftly arrive at a choice.

Whether you're deciding where to go this weekend, what to eat tonight, or what to wear tomorrow, Rand Choice is here to streamline your decision-making process.

Each option comes with its own set of pros and cons. Spending excessive time deliberating over these matters isn't productive. Let Rand Choice be your guide!

Features:

  • Create custom decision sets tailored to your needs.
  • Utilize the Lucky Wheel, Slot Machine, or Treasure Chest to generate your choice effortlessly.

Common Issues

  • App crashes on startup: Ensure you have the latest version of the app and your device's operating system is up to date.
  • Spinner/Slot Machine/Treasure Chest not working: Restart the app and try again. If the problem persists, reinstall the app.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add options to my decision set?
You can add options by tapping the "Add Option" button and entering the details.
Is there a limit to the number of options I can add?
No, you can add as many options as you need to your decision set.
